Experience Our Care Homes

Midas Touch Healthcare offers a warm and welcoming environment for individuals who require round-the-clock care in a homely setting. Our care homes provide a supportive and nurturing atmosphere where residents receive personalized care and attention from our compassionate staff.

Our Approach

We believe in creating a home-like atmosphere where residents feel comfortable, safe, and valued. Our care homes are designed to promote independence and well-being while providing the support and assistance you need to thrive. Our dedicated team of caregivers is committed to ensuring that each resident receives the highest standard of care with dignity and respect.

Services Offered

  • - 24-hour care and supervision by trained and experienced staff

  • - Assistance with activities of daily living, including personal care and grooming

  • - Medication management and administration

  • - Nutritious meals and snacks prepared according to dietary needs and preferences

  • - Engaging in activities and social programs to promote physical and mental well-being

  • - Coordination of healthcare services and appointments

Why Choose Our Care Homes?

  • - Safe and supportive environment with round-the-clock care

  • - Individualized care plans tailored to your unique needs and preferences

  • - Peace of mind for you and your loved ones knowing that you're in good hands

  • - Opportunities for social interaction, engagement, and meaningful connections

  • - Comfortable and homely atmosphere where you can feel at home
