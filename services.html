Welcome to Our
Domiciliary Care Service

At Midas Touch Healthcare, we understand the importance of maintaining independence and comfort while receiving care. Our domiciliary care service is designed to provide personalized support in the familiarity of your own home. Whether you need assistance with daily activities, medication management, or specialized medical support, our dedicated team of caregivers is here to help.

Support Living

We believe that everyone deserves to live life to the fullest, regardless of challenges. Our supported living services are designed to empower individuals with disabilities to lead fulfilling lives in their communities, with the support they need to thrive.

Care Homes

For those who require round-the-clock care in a comfortable, nurturing environment, our care homes offer a place to call home. Our compassionate staff ensures residents receive the highest standard of care, tailored to their individual needs.
